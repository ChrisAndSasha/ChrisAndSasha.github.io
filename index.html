Things to do in Philly

Aside from the barbecue, you may wish to explore Philadelphia and the surrounding areas. Philadelphia is a city rich with American history. Here are some ideas for things to do while you are in town (in addition to finding a good Philly cheese steak).

History

Some of the highlights are the Liberty Belland Independence Hall, but we suggest following the tour on the official Philadelphia website: The Official Trail of Philadelphia's Historic District.

Museums

Barnes Foundation: one of the best collections of impressionist art you can find anywhere.

Rodin Museum: a fantastic museum housing many works by this creative sculptor.

Philadelphia Museum of Art: aside from the art, this museum is also famous for its steps; run up them and unleash your inner Rocky.

Museum of the American Revolution

National Museum of American Jewish History

Flora and Fauna

Tyler Arboretum: all guests have access to the arboretum all day on Sunday, but this can also be a good activity for Saturday. There are miles of hiking trails and nature walks.

Ridley Creek State Park: this is the state park in which Tyler Arboretum is located, and it is also a great option to get out into nature.

Philadelphia Zoo
